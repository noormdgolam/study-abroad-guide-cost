Scholarships and Financial Aid

Destination 35 offers various scholarships and financial aid options to help students fund their education. Here are some of the available options:

  • Merit-based scholarships: Awarded to students with high academic achievement
  • Need-based scholarships: Awarded to students demonstrating financial need
  • Research assistantships: Offered to students pursuing research-based programs
  • Campus jobs: Available to students seeking part-time employment on campus

Students are encouraged to explore these options and consult with financial aid advisors to determine eligibility and application requirements.
