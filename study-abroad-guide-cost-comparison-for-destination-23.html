Tuition Fees

Public universities in Destination 23 typically offer low tuition fees, ranging from $2,000 to $5,000 per semester for undergraduate programs.

Accommodation Costs

Sharing an apartment with fellow students or staying in a hostel can cost between $500 to $1,000 per month, depending on location and amenities.

Travel and Accommodation

Flights from the US or Europe can range from $800 to $2,000 roundtrip, depending on the time of year and availability. Transportation costs within Destination 23 can add $100 to $300 per month.
