Tuition Fees

  • Public Institutions: The tuition fees for public institutions in Destination 89 are relatively low, ranging from $3,000 to $6,000 per year for undergraduate programs and $5,000 to $10,000 per year for graduate programs.
  • Private Institutions: Private institutions in Destination 89 often charge higher tuition fees, ranging from $8,000 to $20,000 per year for undergraduate programs and $12,000 to $25,000 per year for graduate programs.
