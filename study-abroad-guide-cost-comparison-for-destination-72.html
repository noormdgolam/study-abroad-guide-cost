Overall Cost of Living

The overall cost of living at destination 72 is relatively low, with a monthly budget of $1,500 - $2,500 enough to cover living expenses, including food, accommodation, and transportation.

Total Estimated Cost for a Year: $15,000 - $30,000 per year

By understanding the costs involved and planning ahead, you can make the most out of your study abroad experience at destination 72.

Additional Tips

We recommend the following tips to help you save money and make the most of your experience:

  • Live with a roommate: Sharing accommodation with a roommate can help split costs and save you money.
  • Cook your own meals: Eating out can get expensive, so consider cooking your own meals or using a meal ticket at the university cafeteria.
  • Use public transport: Using public transport is an affordable and convenient way to get around the city.
  • Take advantage of campus resources: Many universities offer student health centers, libraries, and other resources that can help you save money.

By following these tips and budgeting accordingly, you can have a successful and enjoyable study abroad experience at destination 72.
